🏏 About CricketBook

CricketBook is a free, real-time ball-by-ball live score sharing tool for local cricket matches. One person scores, everyone else follows along live — that's it. It's built to be fast, simple, and instant.

❌ What CricketBook Does NOT Do

  • ❌ No batsman names or batting scorecards — it does not record who scored what
  • ❌ No bowler names or bowling figures — it does not track who bowled which over
  • ❌ No individual player statistics — no runs, strike rates, wickets per bowler, averages, or maiden overs
  • ❌ No fall of wickets per batsman — it only tracks how many wickets the team has lost
  • ❌ Not a full scorecard app — you cannot produce an end-of-match player summary

If you need full player-level scorecards (batsman runs, bowler wickets etc.), please use a dedicated scorecard app. CricketBook is intentionally team-score-only — keeping it fast and hassle-free.

✅ What CricketBook DOES Do

  • Tracks team scores ball-by-ball — runs (0–6), wickets, extras (wide, no-ball, bye, leg-bye) & dots
  • Shows live running score to spectators in real-time as each ball is entered
  • Works for Quick Matches (one-off games) or Tournaments (multi-match leagues)
  • Calculates Points Table & NRR automatically for league tournaments
  • Lets you share a live link instantly via WhatsApp — no login needed to view the score
  • Supports Unlimited Undo — reverse any ball entry at any time
  • Keeps match history for ~3 months so you can browse past results
  • Works on any phone browser — no app download, no account, 100% free

✨ Features at a Glance

  • 📱 Quick Match — Single match, no signup needed
  • ⚡ Real-time sync — Ball-by-ball cloud updates, spectators see it live instantly
  • 🏆 Tournament Mode — Multiple matches with auto points table & NRR
  • 🔗 Two share links — View link (spectators) & Scorer link (co-scorers with edit access)
  • 💬 WhatsApp sharing — One-tap share of the live link to your group
  • 🔄 Unlimited Undo — Reverse any ball entry anytime, no restrictions
  • 🌓 Dark Mode — Easy on the eyes during evening & night matches
  • 💾 Auto-save — Resume any incomplete match exactly where you left off
  • ⏰ 3-month history — Match records stored for approximately 3 months
  • 🆓 100% Free — No signup, no ads, no payments, ever

🎯 How to Get Started

  1. Go to Scorer — tap “Start Scoring” on the home page
  2. Set up your match — enter team names, number of overs (1–50), max wickets
  3. Score ball by ball — tap: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, Wide, No-ball, Wicket…
  4. Share the live link — send via WhatsApp to your group and they follow along live

🏆 Tournament Points & NRR

Points: Win = 2 pts | Tie = 1 pt | Loss = 0 pts

NRR = (Runs Scored ÷ Overs Faced) − (Runs Conceded ÷ Overs Bowled)

Note: NRR applies only to League Match type, using the ICC standard method. Single-group only is supported.
